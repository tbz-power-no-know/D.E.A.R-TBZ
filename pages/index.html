Drop Everything And Read — Student podcast presentations about books

What is D.E.A.R?

D.E.A.R. stands for Drop Everything And Read. It started as a reading initiative where everyone stops what they're doing and spends time with a book — no phones, no distractions, just reading.

At the Technical Vocational School Zurich, we took this idea further. Students in our ABU (General Education) classes read books across different genres — from crime thrillers to science fiction, biographies to classics — and turned their thoughts into podcast presentations. Each podcast is a personal take on a book: why it resonated, what stood out, and whether you should pick it up yourself.

This website is the home of those podcasts. It's a place to discover new books through the voices of fellow students, find your next great read, and maybe even get inspired to share your own.
